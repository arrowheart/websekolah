About Us


Profile

SMP Negeri 1 Sidoarjo is a state junior high school educational institution located on Jl. Ponti Sidoarjo, Sidoarjo Regency.

This state junior high school was first established in 1952. At this time, SMP Negeri 1 Sidoarjo uses the 2013 SMP learning curriculum. SMP Negeri 1 Sidoarjo has a principal named Matnuri and school operator Anggraini Sartikawati.



School Accreditation

SMP Negeri 1 Sidoarjo has grade A accreditation with a score of 93 (accreditation in 2021) from BAN-S/M (National Accreditation Board) Schools/Madrasas.
